ABOUT


The Supply Chain Management is a centralized monitoring system to optimize supply chain system considering the stock in go-down,containers and retail outlets.The system should also be able to predict the stock requirement of each ration shop based on the local factors such as natural calamities and climatic changes.

 




MISSION


To ensure equitable distribution of the food grains at a fairly low cost to all people especially to the weaker sections of the population. There is a very good network of wholesale and retail outlets for the distribution of rationed articles under the Public Distribution System.





VISION


Ensure all commodities are properly utilized and avoid black market.Make sure that right people are getting sufficient raw materials.System should help the centralized monitoring system to provide adequate stocks to the respecive outletss based on the predictions.
